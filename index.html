Adopt, Don’t Shop

Every dog on this page is waiting for a home, a couch, and a human who doesn’t mind a little extra fur on their clothes. When you adopt, you’re saving a life and gaining a furry chaos gremlin roommate.

Visit your local shelter, talk to staff about temperament and energy level, and find a dog that fits your lifestyle—not just your Instagram aesthetic.

Training

Basic obedience classes help dogs adjust faster in their new homes.

Exercise

Most pups need at least 30–60 minutes of play per day. Zoomies included.

Grooming

Regular brushing keeps coats shiny and tumbleweed fur to a minimum.

Vet Care

Vaccines, checkups, and preventative meds keep tails wagging long-term.

Supplies

Leash, collar, ID tag, comfy bed, and at least one ridiculous squeaky toy.
